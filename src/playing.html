Game info

    Get Ready to Play!

    If you’re doing single-player / practice / campaign, just look at each hand and press any key when you’re ready to move to the next. We’ll be keeping stats for you. For multiplayer it helps if everyone knows a few tips:

    • Players will be shown one hand at a time.
    • The first player to find the right image for that hand should say the name of the image and press any key on the keyboard to claim the hand.
    • The game will indicate which key was pressed first, though most people prefer to give credit to the player who managed to say the name of the image first.
    • Once someone presses a key, everyone can relax and decide who gets to claim the point(s) for that hand.
    • The game will let you add points to the chosen player(s) but keep the total score secret until the end of the game.
    • Everyone then goes on to the next hand until the game is over and the final scores are shown.

    Press Enter to start, Escape to cancel.
