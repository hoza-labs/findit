Tips for printing

  • Use Actual size or 100% scaling when the print dialog offers it.
  • Saving to PDF first is a great way to proof margins, card size, and page count.
  • Photo paper can make colors pop, and lamination helps cards survive a lot of games.
  • Your browser and printer still control paper source, paper type, duplex, and final hardware quality.
